Sylvester: The Talking Cat

Sylvester Cash, also known as Sylvester was loved and missed by all. He was the main protagonist or sometimes the antagonist of Steve Cash's YouTube series, Talking Kitty Cat.

About Sylvester

Sylvester was the cat owned by Steve Cash. Steve Cash got Sylvester from an ad on Craiglist when it was a kitten.

Sylvester also got other companions as Steve adopted Shelby, Gibson, Random Kitty, Gibbyson, and GG. Most of the time Sylvester want to be superior from them.

Unfortunately, Steve Cash passed away after a suicide incident. After that, Sylvester was taken care of by Steve's wife, while Shelby was adopted by Darcy.

After seven months of Steve's death, Sylvester passed the rainbow bridge on December 10, 2020.

It's been almost 3 years since Sylvester and Steve had passed away, still they bring laughter and joy until this day.
